About Me

First and foremost, I love to learn. If someone is knowledgeable about a topic, I am absolutely interested in what they would like to say. Whether it is science, politics, literature, history, etc. I will always be willing to learn and love to understand different perspectives from my own. This is a huge reason I am so excited to be in this field. There is, and always will be, something to learn.

I love to build things. I love to plan an idea and see it to fruition. Obviously I love software development, as I am changing fields to follow my passion, but I also love to build just about anything. I can play with Legos for hour, but I also enjoy adult Legos like woodworking and general house upkeep. While I haven’t been trained in the field for TOO long, I have been playing around with C++ building little simple programs for fun since High School.

I am also a HUGE bleeding heart. I always want to help. I have volunteered with Habitat for Humanity, Boy Scouts of America, Boys and Girls Club, food banks, local and other organizations. I held a leadership role in a volunteer organization while attending UT. I am also an Eagle Scout which further displays my interest in my community as well.

Last, I am an outdoorsman. This one is a little more surprising, but being an Eagle Scout, and generally always pursuing adventure, I love to be with nature just as much as I love a good video game. I particularly enjoy hiking, camping, canoeing, rock-climbing, and just about anything else outside.
